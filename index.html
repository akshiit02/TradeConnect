What We Actually Do

We're an export sourcing broker. We don't manufacture, store, or directly sell products. Our role is to find the right supplier for your requirement and make sure the deal goes smoothly.

Supplier Sourcing

For every enquiry, we identify 2–4 suppliers who match your requirement — price, quality, volume, and timeline. You get options, not a fixed source.

Quality Verification

We coordinate samples and lab reports (COA, test certificates) so you know exactly what you're buying before committing to an order.

Price Negotiation

We negotiate competitive bulk rates using local market knowledge. No hidden charges.

Order Coordination

We act as your communication bridge managing language barriers, time zones, documentation queries, and supplier follow-ups so you don't have to.

Buyer-Seller Matchmaking

We don't just forward contacts we assess fit before making introductions. Supplier capacity, buyer volume, price range, and quality level are matched before anyone's time is wasted.

Shipment Monitoring

Once the order is confirmed, we stay involved tracking progress, ensuring dispatch timelines are met, and flagging any issues early.

Full Transparency Notice

TradeConnect operates solely as a sourcing intermediary. We do not manufacture, stock, or directly sell any products. All transactions are between the buyer and supplier we facilitate and coordinate. Our fee is disclosed before any commitment is made.

Our Process

How It Works

A clear, transparent process — so you know exactly what we do and who handles what at every stage.

01

You Submit Your Requirement

Tell us what you need: product, quantity, target price, destination, and any certifications. We accept enquiries via the form below, email, or WhatsApp.

02

We Identify Suitable Suppliers

We contact 2–4 suppliers from our network who can meet your specs and volume. We ask for pricing, MOQ, and certifications.

03

We Share Honest Quotations

We present you with clear quotations — supplier price, our service margin, and estimated logistics. No hidden figures.

04

Sample & Quality Confirmation

We share the supplier's Certificate of Analysis (COA) and test reports with you directly — so you can verify quality on paper before committing to a full order.

05

Buyer & Supplier Agree Terms

Once you're satisfied, you confirm the order directly with the supplier. We facilitate the purchase order and payment structure.

06

Production, Dispatch & Follow-up

Supplier produces and ships. We monitor progress, assist with documentation, and stay involved until your shipment is on the way.
